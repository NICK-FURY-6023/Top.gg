GALAXY: The Ultimate Music Bot for Your Discord

Welcome to GALAXY – the all-in-one music bot that brings unparalleled sound quality and flexibility to your Discord server. With support for over 18 platforms, including seamless Spotify integration, GALAXY is the best choice for anyone looking to elevate their server’s music experience without extra fees.

What is GALAXY?

GALAXY is a feature-packed yet user-friendly music bot designed specifically for Discord servers. Whether you're hosting a casual hangout or a large community, GALAXY offers robust features that make listening easy, enjoyable, and accessible for all.

Why Choose GALAXY?

  • 18+ Streaming Platforms Support: Access a vast music library through 18 supported streaming platforms, providing more options for everyone’s taste.
  • Special Control Panel: The user-friendly control panel allows you to fully manage the music, giving you control over every beat.
  • DJ System for Secure Playback: Prevent unauthorized users from interfering with the music by activating DJ mode and assigning DJ permissions via Discord roles or specific users using /dj_role @role and /dj_uset @user.
  • Play Custom Songs from Your Device: Enjoy ultimate flexibility by playing your custom MP3 files directly from your device.
  • Personalized User Profiles: Track your listening habits with custom profiles and view your GALAXY stats for a personalized experience.
  • 24/7 Mode: Enjoy non-stop music with a unique 24/7 mode, ensuring GALAXY stays in the voice channel as long as you like.
  • Playlist System: Effortlessly create and manage playlists, organizing your favorite songs for any event or mood.
  • High-Quality Audio: Experience superior sound quality with crystal-clear audio that enhances every note and beat.
  • Fully Configurable: GALAXY adapts to your server’s needs with various customization options, making it a seamless addition to any community.
  • Future-Ready with New Features Coming Soon: GALAXY is always evolving, with exciting new features on the way. Stay tuned for continuous updates that keep the music experience fresh and full of surprises!

Advanced Controls & Customization

/changelavalink: If you ever encounter playback issues or delays, simply use the /changelavalink command to switch to a different Lavalink server, ensuring uninterrupted music.

/setvoicestatus: Customize GALAXY’s voice channel presence with /setvoicestatus. Choose from auto, permanent, and default modes or set unique statuses to align with your server’s vibe.

Player Modes: Choose from various player modes like Mimi Mode and Pro Mode to customize your listening experience further.

Commands Overview

⠂Miscellaneous Commands (28)

  • about - Displays bot information.
  • help - Shows available commands and how to use them.
  • invite - Provides an invite link for the bot.
  • panel - Access the admin control panel.
  • setprefix - Changes the bot's command prefix.
  • summon - Summons the bot to the voice channel.

⠂Music Commands (37)

  • play - Play music from a URL or search query.
  • pause - Pauses the current track.
  • skip - Skips the current track.
  • autoplay - Toggles autoplay.
  • nightcore - Enables nightcore mode.
  • songrequestthread - Creates a song request thread for users.
  • reverse - Reverses the current queue.
  • savequeue - Saves the current queue for later.

⠂Settings Commands (9)

  • adddjrole - Adds a DJ role to control music.
  • changeskin - Changes the bot's skin theme.
  • customskin - Sets a custom skin for the bot.
  • listenalong - Lets users listen along with the bot.

To learn more about each command, type /help in your server to get detailed instructions on how to use GALAXY to its fullest!
